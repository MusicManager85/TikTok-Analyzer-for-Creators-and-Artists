Visual format, captions, and posting

What is on screen

The dominant visual is a DJ behind Pioneer decks facing a crowd, often at an outdoor festival or rooftop venue, with dramatic colored lighting (green, red, or blue) filling the frame. Crowd reaction shots and wide festival establishing visuals appear in nearly every top-10 video, grounding the music in a physical, emotional live experience. Text overlays are burned in early and paired with visuals that immediately signal scale and energy rather than explaining anything.
